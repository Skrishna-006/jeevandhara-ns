## 🎯 THE KEY INNOVATION

### Case-Specific Contribution Tracking ⭐

**Problem Solved:**  
Universities want to see exactly which of their contributions went to which specific medical cases.

**Solution Provided:**  
Shows complete traceability from contribution → case → patient → treatment type

**Example:**

```
Dr. Rajesh Kumar contributed ₹25,000 to Meena Devi's Cardiac Surgery (Case JD-2847)
```

**Impact:**

- Transparency for donors
- Accountability in fund usage
- Stronger donor-patient relationships
- Clear impact measurement
